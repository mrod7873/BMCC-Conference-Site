About Me

I'm Michael Rodriguez and I'm a current BMCC student whose major is Multimedia Programming. I enjoy coding websites since I can bring my technical expertise to the field and I have a natural knack for it. Also, my business background aids me in organizing and managing content that's geared towards a specific audience when it comes to making web sites. I love web design since I can use both my technical proficiency and my business knowledge to make dynamic websites that aid me in making them more engaging to audiences worldwide.
